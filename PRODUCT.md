# PRODUCT.md

## Product Purpose
Personal portfolio for a computer science student, aimed at internship and
job hunting. Recruiters and hiring managers should grasp who the author is,
what they build, and how to reach them within one scroll.

## Register
brand

## Users
- Recruiters and hiring managers skimming quickly (primary)
- Fellow students and engineers browsing projects (secondary)

## Brand
A CS portfolio curated like a manga storefront. Projects are "volumes" on a
shelf; pages read like chapters. Voice words: inky, printed, collected.

- Palette: "Paper & Ink" warm monochrome (cream papers, near-black inks) with
  a single vermillion hanko-stamp accent used sparingly (badges, brand dot).
- Type: Space Mono for display/labels/metadata, Rubik for body copy. This
  pairing is committed brand identity from the original comp; preserve it.
- Microcopy stays in the manga register: "End of Chapter 1. Thanks for
  reading.", "To be continued…", volume numbers, edition labels.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS landing pages (gradient heroes, glassmorphism, metric cards)
- Anime-character illustration; the manga influence is print design, not art
- Dark "terminal hacker" developer-portfolio clichés
